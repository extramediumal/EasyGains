## Description

Talk to your phone. Track your macros. That's it.

EasyGains is the macro tracker that finally gets out of your way. Just say what you ate -- "two eggs and some toast" -- and Coach AL handles the rest. No barcode scanning. No scrolling through a database of 47 different brands of Greek yogurt. Just your voice and an AI that actually understands food.

TRACK MACROS BY TALKING
Open the app, tap the mic, say what you ate. Coach AL logs it in seconds. You can be vague ("a big bowl of pasta") or specific ("6oz chicken breast, 200g rice"). Either way, it works.

THE MACROTRIANCE -- YOUR DAILY SNAPSHOT
Forget pie charts. The MacroTriforce shows your protein, carbs, and fat balance at a glance. Green means you're dialed in. It takes half a second to understand, which is the whole point.

COACH AL -- YOUR SLIGHTLY OPINIONATED AI COACH
Coach AL lives in your notification bar and has thoughts about your choices. Hit your protein goal? He's hyped. Skip lunch and then eat 2000 calories at 9pm? He's going to mention it. He's not mean about it, but he's not going to pretend that was a great strategy either.

Coach AL nudges you with timely reminders, calls out patterns in your eating, and genuinely celebrates your wins. Think of him as that one friend who actually tracks macros and won't shut up about protein -- except he's useful.

BUILT FOR PEOPLE WHO HATE TRACKING
If you've tried MyFitnessPal and quit (like everyone), this is for you. EasyGains strips out everything that makes traditional macro tracking a chore:

- No barcode scanning required
- No manually assembling meals from ingredient lists
- No logging in to a social feed you never asked for
- No 47-step onboarding flow

Just voice in, macros out.

WEEKLY TRENDS WITHOUT THE OVERWHELM
See your week at a glance. Spot patterns. Notice that you always undershoot protein on Wednesdays. Fix it or don't -- but at least now you know.

PRIVACY FIRST
Your food data stays on your device. We don't sell it. We don't analyze it for ad targeting. Coach AL judges your meals, not your identity.

Whether you're bulking, cutting, or just trying to eat enough protein for once in your life, EasyGains makes tracking so fast you might actually stick with it this time.
