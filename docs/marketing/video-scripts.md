## Production Notes

**Content calendar suggestion:** Release 3-4 per week, rotating content types. Lead with Coach AL and voice demo content (highest share potential), mix in educational and behind-the-scenes to build trust.

**Filming tips:**
- Record voice demos with actual screen captures, not mockups -- authenticity matters
- Coach AL clips work best as screen recordings with face cam in corner
- MacroTriforce content needs smooth screen recording -- use a tripod or screen capture tool
- Behind-the-scenes dev content should look casual and unpolished on purpose
- All face-to-camera content should be in natural lighting, no ring light influencer aesthetic

**Sound/music:**
- Voice demos: no music or very low background music so the voice input is audible
- MacroTriforce content: trending satisfying/ASMR-adjacent sounds
- Coach AL content: works best with no music, just deadpan delivery
- Comparison content: use trending audio if applicable
