# Mirofish Simulation Results — EasyGains Marketing Campaign

**Date:** 2026-03-25
**Source:** Mirofish agent simulation (100+ agents, 10 persona types, 6 channels, 4-week simulated campaign)
**Seed doc:** `Mirofish/easygains-marketing-strategy-seed.md`

## Channel Effectiveness Ranking

| Channel | Downloads | Pro Conversion | 30-Day Retention | Best For |
|---------|-----------|---------------|-----------------|----------|
| Reddit organic | Moderate | High | ~60% (highest) | Quality users, limited budget |
| Twitter/X (Coach AL) | Moderate | ~25% (highest) | High | Personality-driven conversion |
| TikTok / IG Reels | Highest volume | Moderate | Moderate | Awareness, curiosity downloads |
| Influencer partnerships | High | Moderate | Lower (churn risk) | Social proof acceleration |
| ASO (App Store) | Low but steady | High intent | High | Long-term organic discovery |

## Key Findings

### Coach AL is the marketing asset
- Coach AL's humor drove the highest free-to-Pro conversion (25% on Twitter)
- Users shared screenshots and quotes organically — personality sells better than utility
- "Coach AL roasts your food choices" outperformed "log meals in 4 seconds by voice" as a hook
- The personality functions as a standalone marketing channel through organic sharing

### Reddit produces the best users
- 60% still active after 30 days (highest retention of any channel)
- Authentic indie dev story + community discussion = deep engagement
- Self-selected users convert to Pro at higher rates
- Free to execute — best channel for limited budgets

### TikTok is the volume play
- "Wait, that's it?" reaction to 4-second voice demo drives curiosity downloads
- Raw/unpolished creator-style content outperforms polished ads
- High volume but uncertain quality — need to pair with strong onboarding

### Messages that resonated (ranked)
1. "Coach AL roasts your food choices" — strongest emotional response
2. Real user stories and success stories — trust builder
3. MFP comparison / "I quit MFP" narrative — identity trigger
4. "Log meals in 4 seconds by voice" — direct but less shareable

### Messages that underperformed
- Pure utility messaging without personality fell flat
- Clinical or data-heavy positioning lost to humor/personality

## Budget Recommendation ($500)

- **Reddit organic:** $0 (time investment only) — seed first, pre-launch
- **TikTok content creation:** ~$100-200 (basic equipment, maybe one promoted post)
- **Coach AL Twitter:** $0 (organic, ongoing)
- **ASO optimization:** $0 (one-time effort)
- **Reserve:** $300 for amplifying whatever goes viral

## Recommended Launch Sequence

1. **Week 0 (pre-launch):** Reddit seeding — "What made you quit tracking macros?" discussions, indie dev story
2. **Week 1 (launch day):** TikTok/Reels push — 4-second demo videos, MFP comparison
3. **Week 1+ (ongoing):** Coach AL Twitter account — daily personality posts, engagement
4. **Week 2+:** Influencer outreach only if organic channels show traction
5. **Month 2-3:** SEO content starts ranking, compounding organic discovery

## Competitive Note

- MacroFactor flagged as main competitor for data-driven users
- EasyGains wins on personality + simplicity, not on data depth
- Don't try to out-feature MacroFactor — out-personality them

## Simulation Caveats

- Agent-based simulation, not real user data
- Conversion rates and retention numbers are directional, not precise
- Useful for relative channel comparison and message testing, not absolute forecasting
